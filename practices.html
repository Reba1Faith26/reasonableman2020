    • Our Practice Areas

    Commecial Law

    Contracts, licenses, procurement, tenders, franchising, privacy, risk management and regulatory advice. Commercial law is about daily operational legal requirements of a commercial client. It is at the heart of a corporate entity and servicing it is at the heart of what we do. We serve diverse clients in a variety of industries. For clients who have in-house legal teams, we act as extensions of those teams. As part of the total corporate process we put great effort into learning our clients’ businesses in order to offer them tailor-made and innovative legal solutions. What does “everyday” mean? For us it means drafting, reviewing and negotiating a number of contracts, setting clients up with manageable, plain language set of standard contracts that are custom drafted to the clients’ specific commercial risk profile. We advise clients on the legal aspects of all commercial transactions, ensuring that clients comply with all the rules and regulations that govern their business/industries

    Litigation & Dispute

    Litigation in all the Courts of South Africa and arbitrations, mediations, letters of demands and regulatory advice. Disputes are an inevitable part of commercial activity. Our approach is that litigation should be resorted to as a last course. We have experienced lawyers in the field of litigation if that is the last course to resort to.

    Corporate Law

    Corporate law is mostly about deals, Initial Public Offerings, takeovers, mergers, trade sales, joint ventures and capital raisings. Such kind of deals need an experienced attorney to advise on both the structure and from a corporate governance point of view. Each deal is unique, the Companies Act is huge and complex and deal making requires acquired knowledge and experience. We have an experienced corporate lawyer to help you deal with the complexities of deal structure, governance, due diligence, transaction documentation and protecting your interests post deal.

    Energy, Mining and Infrastructure

    Joint venture agreements, sale and purchase agreements, drilling agreements, service agreements Undoubtedly, mining, energy and infrastructure development are primary essential parts of the South African economy. We have expertise in advising clients involved in the mining industry. We provide advice on mining projects and provide dispute resolution advice when the necessity arises. We advise clients in the infrastructure and transport industries on the legal issues which they may face. Disputes often arise and we advise clients on the resolution the issues.

    Property law

    Commercial leasing, conveyancing, property development Our specialized lawyers looks after all our clients’ property needs, from property transfers, estate transfers, commercial and residential sale agreements and related property searches and queries.

    Criminal law

    24 hours bail applications, drunk driving matters, dealing in drugs, assault, theft, illegal possession of firearms, murder, armed robbery and much more We help our clients in all areas of concern from obtaining after hours bail applications, to the investigation of the alleged offence and conducting the trial. Value added services such as assistance in the returning of seized properties from the SAPS and the like is offered. We also offer appeal services, be that applying for leave to appeal against unwanted convictions or conducting the appeals in the high court against unwanted convictions and undesired sentences.
